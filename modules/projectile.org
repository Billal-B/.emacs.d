* Configuration
** General
   The variable ~projectile-completion-system~ must be set in =helm.org= or =ivy.org=.
#+begin_src emacs-lisp
(projectile-mode +1)
(setq projectile-enable-caching t)
(setq projectile-indexing-method 'hybrid)
#+end_src
